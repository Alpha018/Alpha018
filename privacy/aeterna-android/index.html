Privacy Policy — Aeterna Android

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Aeterna Android ("Aeterna", "the app") handles data when used as a mobile client for a user-controlled self-hosted Aeterna server.

1. Controller and Contact

Developer: Tomás Alegre
Privacy contact: android@alphacode.cl

2. Scope

This policy applies to the Android application published on Google Play as Aeterna Android, package cl.alpha018.aeterna.

3. Data We Process

  • Connection and account data: self-hosted server URL, authentication/session token, and connection preferences.
  • Core app data: switches/triggers, recipients, reminders, heartbeat-related metadata, and message/farewell configuration.
  • Attachments: files explicitly selected by the user for trigger-related workflows.
  • Diagnostics and telemetry (Firebase): app events, crash reports, performance traces, installation/app instance identifiers, and device/app metadata processed by Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Performance Monitoring.

4. Why We Process Data

  • Deliver the app's primary functionality.
  • Synchronize data with the user-provided self-hosted server.
  • Send reminders and local alerts configured by the user.
  • Monitor reliability and improve stability/performance.

5. Legal Bases (where applicable)

  • Contract/performance: to provide the requested app service.
  • Legitimate interests: to maintain app security, reliability, and performance.
  • Consent/settings: for optional telemetry controls when enabled by the user.

6. Security and Transport

  • Data at rest: sensitive local data is stored using local encryption mechanisms.
  • Data in transit: Aeterna can connect to self-hosted servers using either HTTP or HTTPS, depending on user/server configuration.
  • Security recommendation: use HTTPS/TLS whenever possible. HTTP traffic is not encrypted in transit.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be disclosed only as needed to:

  • Communicate with the user's self-hosted Aeterna server to provide core features.
  • Use service providers required for app operation and diagnostics, such as Google Firebase.
  • Comply with valid legal obligations.

8. Firebase and User Choice

The app includes Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, and Performance Monitoring. Aeterna provides an in-app setting to enable or disable analytics/telemetry collection for these services.

For more information about Firebase privacy and security, see firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

9. Retention and Deletion

  • Local app data is retained while the app is installed, unless removed by user actions such as logout, clearing app data, or uninstall.
  • Depending on Android backup/account settings, some app data may also be included in system-level backup or device transfer mechanisms.
  • Server-side data retention is managed by the operator of the self-hosted server instance.

10. International Data Transfers

Depending on your server location and third-party service infrastructure (for example, Firebase), data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence, subject to the safeguards provided by those services.

11. Account Deletion

If your deployment allows account creation, account deletion requests must be handled by the operator of the corresponding self-hosted Aeterna server. Users should contact their server operator or use the deletion mechanism made available by that deployment.

12. User Requests

For privacy questions or data-related requests, contact: android@alphacode.cl.

13. Children

Aeterna is not specifically directed to children.

14. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect legal, technical, or product changes. The latest version will remain available at this URL.
